About GradeIQ

GradeIQ is an intelligent student analytics platform that leverages Machine Learning and predictive modeling to estimate academic outcomes. The system evaluates learning behavior, attendance trends, study habits, and engagement factors to generate performance predictions and actionable recommendations for academic improvement.


GradeIQ helps students understand their academic performance by analyzing important study-related factors and generating predicted results. The system provides personalized improvement suggestions, enables students to track their prediction history, and supports data-driven decision making for better academic outcomes. The platform is designed to be simple, interactive, and useful for students who want to monitor and improve their learning progress.
